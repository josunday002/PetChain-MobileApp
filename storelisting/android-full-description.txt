🏥 PET HEALTH RECORDS MANAGER

PetChain is your all-in-one pet healthcare companion. Store vaccination records, track medications, manage vet appointments, and share pet profiles — all secured by blockchain technology on the Stellar network.

🌟 CORE FEATURES

🔒 SECURE MEDICAL VAULT
- Encrypted storage of all pet health data (AES-256)
- Blockchain-verified immutable records — tamper-evident audit trail
- Export PDFs instantly for vet visits or insurance claims
- HIPAA-compliant architecture for US clinics

📸 QR CODE SCANNER & SHARER
- Scan any pet's QR code to view their medical profile
- Generate shareable QR codes for your own pets
- One-tap sharing to veterinarians during emergencies
- Access pet info without needing the PetChain app

💊 MEDICATION & VACCINATION TRACKER
- Smart notifications never miss a dose
- Set complex schedules (morning/evening, every other day, etc.)
- Track medication history and refill dates
- Low stock alerts before you run out
- Vaccination tracker with due date reminders

📅 VET APPOINTMENT SCHEDULER
- Calendar integration for all appointments
- Reminders 24h and 1h before visits
- Store clinic contact info and directions
- Post-visit notes and follow-up tasks

🚨 EMERGENCY SOS BUTTON
- One-tap SOS from lock screen (no unlock needed)
- Auto-sends pet profile, GPS location, and emergency contacts
- Configurable alert methods (SMS, email, phone call)
- Critical medical info displayed even when phone is locked

📈 HEALTH METRICS & SCORING
- Weight, activity, and vital tracking over time
- Visual health score (A–F) based on multiple factors
- Trend analysis to catch health issues early
- Compare metrics across multiple pets

👥 MULTI-PET HOUSEHOLD SUPPORT
- Add unlimited pets with individual profiles
