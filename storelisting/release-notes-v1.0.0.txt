Version 1.0.0 — Meet PetChain!

Welcome to the official launch of PetChain! We're thrilled to help you take better care of your pets.

🌟 FIRST RELEASE FEATURES

💾 Medical Records Management
- Store vaccination history, illnesses, surgeries, allergies
- Upload lab results, vet notes, and imaging as PDFs/images
- Organized timeline view of all medical events
- Export records as PDF for vets or insurance

💊 Medication Tracker
- Create custom medication schedules (once daily, BID, TID, etc.)
- Push notifications with snooze/dismiss options
- Track completion status and history
- Medication inventory tracking with refill alerts

📅 Appointment Scheduler
- Add upcoming vet visits with date, time, location
- Set custom reminders (1 day, 1 hour before)
- View appointments in list and calendar formats
- Store clinic contact info and directions

📱 QR Code Sharing
- Generate QR codes for each pet profile
- Scan QR codes to view other pets' medical info
- Time-limited access (24h default, customizable)
- Revoke access anytime from your device

🚨 Emergency SOS
- Dedicated SOS button accessible from lock screen
- Auto-alerts pre-configured emergency contacts
- Shares GPS location, pet info, and medical notes
- Works even when phone is locked

📊 Health Dashboard
- Visual health score based on multiple metrics
- Weight, activity, and vital sign tracking
- Trend graphs to spot health changes early
- Multi-pet comparison views

🔐 Security & Privacy
- End-to-end encryption (AES-256)
- Blockchain verification on Stellar network
- Biometric authentication (Face ID/fingerprint)
- GDPR-compliant data export & deletion tools
- No ads, no data selling, no tracking

🌐 Multi-Language Support
- English and Spanish fully localized
- All text, dates, and number formats adapted
- More languages planned for future releases

📱 Optimized for Mobile
- Works completely offline (syncs when online)
- Permission explanations for each feature
- Adaptive layout for phones and tablets
- Dark mode support (follows system theme)

This is just the beginning! Our roadmap includes:
- Community features (pet parent social network)
- Integration with veterinary practice management software
- Pet insurance claims submission
- Wearable device sync (FitBark, Whistle)
- Telehealth vet consultations
- AI-powered symptom checker

Thank you for trusting PetChain with your pet's health information. We're committed to continuous improvement based on your feedback.

Please rate us in the App Store and share PetChain with fellow pet parents!
