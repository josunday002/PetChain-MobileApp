PetChain is a comprehensive pet healthcare app that revolutionizes how you manage your furry friend's medical information. Built with blockchain technology for maximum security and reliability.

KEY FEATURES:

🔒 SECURE MEDICAL RECORDS
• Store all pet health data in one secure location
• Blockchain-verified immutable records on Stellar network
• HIPAA-compliant data protection
• Export records instantly as PDF for vet visits or insurance

📱 QR CODE SCANNER
• Scan any pet's QR code to instantly view their medical profile
• Share your pet's profile via QR for quick vet access
• Instant identification at emergency clinics

💊 MEDICATION REMINDERS
• Never miss a dose with smart push notifications
• Set custom schedules for each medication
• Track medication history and refill dates
• Low stock alerts before running out

📅 APPOINTMENT MANAGEMENT
• Schedule and track all vet appointments
• Receive reminders before visits
• View complete appointment history with notes
• One-tap clinic directions

🚨 EMERGENCY SOS
• One-tap emergency button from lock screen
• Pre-configured emergency contacts instantly notified
• Share location and pet details via SMS/email
• Critical info accessible without unlocking phone

👥 MULTI-PET SUPPORT
• Manage unlimited pets (Premium) or 1 pet (Free)
• Separate profiles with individual health metrics
• Add dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, and other species
• Custom pet profiles with photos

📊 HEALTH DASHBOARD
• Visual health scoring system (A–F grade)
• Track weight, activity, and vitals over time
• Identify trends and potential issues early
• Generate comprehensive health reports

🌐 OFFLINE-FIRST DESIGN
• Full functionality without internet connection
• Automatic secure sync when back online
• Access records anywhere, anytime
• No cloud dependency for core features

🔐 PRIVACY & SECURITY
• Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
• AES-256 encryption for all stored data
• GDPR compliance with data export/delete
• Certificate pinning to prevent MITM attacks
• You own your data — never sold to third parties
